# Beginner Level:
```
Arithmetic: Introduce basic arithmetic concepts, including addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.
Fractions and Decimals: Introduce the concept of fractions and decimals, and how to add, subtract, multiply, and divide them.
Geometry: Introduce basic geometry concepts, including shapes, angles, and measurements.
Algebra: Introduce basic algebraic concepts, including variables, equations, and inequalities.
Probability and Statistics: Introduce the basics of probability and statistics, including probability theory, random variables, and data analysis.
```
# Intermediate Level:
```
Advanced Algebra: Cover advanced topics in algebra, including quadratic equations, functions, and systems of equations.
Trigonometry: Cover the basics of trigonometry, including the relationships between angles and sides in triangles.
Calculus: Introduce the basics of calculus, including derivatives and integrals.
Number Theory: Cover the basics of number theory, including prime numbers, factorization, and modular arithmetic.
Linear Algebra: Introduce the basics of linear algebra, including vectors, matrices, and linear transformations.
```
# Advanced Level:
```
Differential Equations: Introduce the basics of differential equations, including first-order and second-order equations.
Real Analysis: Cover advanced topics in mathematical analysis, including limits, continuity, and differentiability.
Abstract Algebra: Introduce the basics of abstract algebra, including group theory and ring theory.
Topology: Introduce the basics of topology, including the concepts of continuity and convergence.
Probability Theory: Cover advanced topics in probability theory, including random processes, Markov chains, and stochastic calculus.
```
# God Tier:
```
Complex Analysis: Introduce the basics of complex analysis, including complex functions, complex integration, and the Cauchy-Riemann equations.
Algebraic Geometry: Cover the basics of algebraic geometry, including algebraic curves and surfaces.
Number Theory: Cover advanced topics in number theory, including Diophantine equations, elliptic curves, and the Riemann Hypothesis.
Differential Geometry: Cover advanced topics in differential geometry, including curvature, geodesics, and Riemannian manifolds.
Partial Differential Equations: Cover advanced topics in partial differential equations, including the heat equation, wave equation, and Navier-Stokes equations.
```
# Projects above God Tier:
```
Cryptography: Develop a mathematical model for encryption and decryption algorithms and implement them in code.
Machine Learning: Develop a mathematical understanding of machine learning algorithms, including linear regression, logistic regression, and neural networks.
Mathematical Modeling: Develop mathematical models for real-world problems, including predicting the spread of diseases, financial forecasting, and climate modeling.
Optimization: Develop mathematical models for optimization problems, including linear programming, nonlinear programming, and convex optimization.
Chaos Theory: Explore the basics of chaos theory, including chaotic systems, fractals, and the butterfly effect.
```
